Economics is the study of how individuals, businesses, and governments make decisions about the allocation of scarce resources to satisfy their diverse needs and wants. It explores the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services, delving into how markets operate and how they can fail. Economists utilize various models and theories to analyze and predict economic behaviors, aiming to optimize outcomes and devise solutions to pressing challenges. At its core, economics seeks to understand and improve the wellbeing of societies through efficient and equitable resource management.
